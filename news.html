Something about current bills and articles about DC

The most recent bill that has been voted on regarding this issue is H.R 51. This bill provides for admission into the United States of the state of Washington, Douglass Commonwealth, composed of most of the territory of the District of Columbia. The commonwealth shall be admitted to the Union on an equal footing with the other states.

This video created by Vox and posted to Youtube gives some plenty of context on the long battle for representation in DC.

This is the official DC statehood website. On it, the people of DC make the compelling argument in favor of their great region to be admitted into the Union as the 51st state.

Sense taking back control of the House of Representatives, one of the issues that Democrats have pushed for recently is passing legislation on DC statehood. With having a such narrow control in the House, Democrats have been able to pass this bill.
